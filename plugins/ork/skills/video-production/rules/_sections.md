# Rule Categories

## 1. Pre-Production (preproduction) — CRITICAL — 5 rules

Planning, scripting, and structuring video content before recording.

- `preproduction-storyboard.md` — AIDA framework, scene templates, shot planning
- `preproduction-narration.md` — Scene-by-scene scripts, timing markers, WPM pacing
- `preproduction-hooks.md` — 12 proven hook patterns for scroll-stopping intros
- `preproduction-recipes.md` — Content type recipes (skill demo, agent demo, tutorial, plugin)
- `preproduction-pacing.md` — Video rhythm, attention curves, platform-specific timing

## 2. Recording (recording) — HIGH — 2 rules

Capturing terminal sessions and creating animated visualizations.

- `recording-terminal.md` — VHS tape format, asciinema recording, CC simulation
- `recording-manim.md` — Manim animations for workflow and architecture visualization

## 3. Composition (composition) — HIGH — 4 rules

Assembling video elements into final compositions with Remotion.

- `composition-remotion.md` — Remotion composition patterns, folder organization, animation presets
- `composition-scenes.md` — Scene intro cards, transitions, timing patterns
- `composition-callouts.md` — Debug grids, coordinate systems, callout positioning
- `composition-thumbnails.md` — Thumbnail formulas, first-frame optimization, platform requirements

## 4. Audio (audio) — HIGH — 4 rules

Music selection, sound effects, mixing, and text-to-speech narration.

- `audio-music.md` — Music matching matrix, BPM guidelines, mood-to-genre mapping
- `audio-mixing.md` — ffmpeg filters, ducking patterns, volume balancing, LUFS normalization
- `audio-elevenlabs.md` — ElevenLabs TTS API integration, voice selection, cost optimization
- `audio-sfx.md` — Sound effect timing, UI sounds, transition audio cues

## 5. Visual Effects (visual) — MEDIUM — 4 rules

Advanced visual elements for Remotion compositions.

- `visual-3d.md` — Three.js 3D scenes, CSS-based 3D, camera animations
- `visual-data-viz.md` — StatCounter, ProgressRing, BarChart, LineChart components
- `visual-lottie.md` — After Effects Lottie integration, animated emojis, Rive runtime
- `visual-effects.md` — ParticleBackground, MeshGradient, GlowOrbs, scene transitions

## 6. Captions & Output (output) — MEDIUM — 2 rules

Subtitles, captions, and final rendering for distribution.

- `output-captions.md` — TikTokCaption, KaraokeCaption, TypingCaption, subtitle generation
- `output-showcase.md` — Showcase templates, multi-format rendering, layer stack composition
