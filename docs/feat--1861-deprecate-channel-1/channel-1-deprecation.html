#1861 — Deprecate channel 1 M141 · last item

Every hook event is currently POSTed twice. This retires the duplicate sender.
⚠️
OrchestKit ships two emitters that POST to the same downstream endpoint. Both fire in parallel, so every event is delivered twice. That already cost a 61% rate-limit reject rate (2026-05-13) and forced a per-session bucketing workaround server-side whose only job is absorbing the duplication. Channel 4 is now canonical — channel 1 has no reason to exist.

Channel 4 became canonical when M141 landed: the contract is published (@orchestkit/hook-contract + orchestkit-hook-contract on PyPI), the platform consumer verifies its HMAC end-to-end (Yonatan-HQ/platform#8206), and openapi/sink.yaml describes the ingest endpoint.
