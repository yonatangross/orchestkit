The honest finding

⚠️ Phase 2's real surface is narrower than "wire all 6." Most "mis-triggered" agents are either already-wired (security-layer), async-incompatible (component-curator), or would need a brand-new spawn surface introduced into a guidance skill (monitoring, git-ops, design-system). The adversarial pass prevented 5 cosmetic wires. The remaining activation lever is Phase 4 (advisor enforce + prompt nudge) and accepting that some agents are legitimately low-use.
